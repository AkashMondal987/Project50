<div class="camel"><h2>Camel</h2>

<p>Step 1) Fold the paper in half.</p>

<p>Step 2) Fold the paper in half again.</p>

<p>Step 3) Open the top flap of paper over to the right.</p>

<p>Step 4) Squash Fold this flap down.</p>

<p>Step 5) Turn the paper over.</p>

<p>Step 6) Open the top flap of paper over to the left and Squash Fold it down.</p>

<p>Step 7) Fold and Unfold the sides and the top along the dotted lines. Youâ€™ll use these creases in the next step.</p>

<p>Step 8) Open up the top layer of paper and fold it up using the crease from the previous step.</p>

<p>Step 9) Fold in both sides along the existing creases making a Petal Fold.</p>

<p>Step 10) Turn the paper over.</p>

<p>Step 11) Repeat steps 7-9 on this side.</p>

<p>Step 12) Fold both sides to the centre along the dotted lines. Then repeat on the other side.</p>

<p>Step 13) Inside Reverse Fold up both sides of the paper just like in a paper crane.</p>

<p>Step 14) Reverse Fold the back part down to make the back legs.</p>

<p>Step 15) Fold the top flap of paper down and repeat on the other side to make the front legs.</p>

<p>Step 16) Reverse Fold the tip down to make the head.</p>

<p>Step 17) Inside Reverse Fold the tip of the head.</p>

<p>Step 18) Fold the back of the leg inside the model along the dotted line. Repeat on the other side.</p>

<div class="chameleon"><h2>Chameleon</h2>

<p>Step 1) Fold and unfold the paper in half both ways. Then fold the top and bottom to the centre.</p>

<p>Step 2) Fold and unfold both sides to the centre.</p>

<p>Step 3) Fold all the corners down along the diagonal dotted lines.</p>

<p>Step 4) Open up each corner and Squash Fold them flat. See the next step to see how each corner looks after the Squash Fold.</p>

<p>Step 5) Turn the paper over.</p>

<p>Step 6) Fold the edges of the paper down along the dotted lines.</p>

<p>Step 7) Turn the paper over.</p>

<p>Step 8) Fold the 4 flaps of paper out along the dotted lines.</p>

<p>Step 9) Fold the paper in half over to the back.</p>

<p>Step 10) Inside Reverse Fold the paper up along the dotted line.</p>

<p>Step 11) Outside Reverse Fold the other side of the model down along the dotted line to form the tail.</p>

<p>Step 12) Make another Outside Reverse Fold on the end of the tail.</p>

<div class="pigeon"><h2>Pigeon</h2>

<p>Step 1)Fold the paper in half and then unfold it.</p>

<p>Step 2)Fold the paper in half the other way.</p>

<p>Step 3)Fold both layers of paper to the right along the dotted line.</p>

<p>Step 4)Fold the top layer of paper to the left along the dotted line.</p>

<p>Step 5)Fold the model in half down along the dotted line.</p>

<p>Step 6)Fold the top flap of paper up along the dotted line and then repeat on the other side.</p>

<p>Step 7)Fold the paper along the dotted line and then unfold it.</p>

<p>Step 8)Make an Inside Reverse Fold using the crease from the previous step.</p>

<div class="teddy"><h2>Teddy Bear</h2>

<p>Step 1) Fold and unfold the paper in half both ways.</p>

<p>Step 2) Fold both sides in to the centre along the dotted lines.</p>

<p>Step 3) Fold both sides in to the centre along the dotted lines.</p>

<p>Step 4) Open up the paper completely.</p>

<p>Step 5) Fold the paper over along the dotted line.</p>

<p>Step 6) Fold the paper back out over to the left.</p>

<p>Step 7) Fold the right side of the paper over along the dotted line and then fold it back out like on the other side.</p>

<p>Step 8) Make a small fold on each side along the dotted lines.</p>

<p>Step 9) Open up the paper along the dotted lines. Lift it up and push it flat. Look at the next diagram to see the final position of this fold.</p>

<p>Step 10) Fold the top of the top layer of paper down along the dotted line.</p>

<p>Step 11) Fold both sides in along the dotted lines.</p>

<p>Step 12) Fold the top of the paper down along the dotted line.</p>

<p>Step 13) Fold the paper underneath along both the dotted lines.</p>

<p>Step 14) Make two Pleat Folds to form the ears.</p>

<p>Step 15) Fold the paper in half a little bit to make the model a bit 3D.</p>

<div class="panda"><h2>Panda</h2>

<p>Step 1) Fold and unfold the paper in half both ways.</p>

<p>Step 2) Fold both sides in along the dotted lines.</p>

<p>Step 3) Fold and unfold the bottom and top along the dotted lines.</p>

<p>Step 4) Fold the paper behind along the dotted line.</p>

<p>Step 5) Turn the paper over.</p>

<p>Step 6) Fold the top of the paper down along the dotted line.</p>

<p>Step 7) Fold the paper up along the dotted line.</p>

<p>Step 8) Fold and unfold the paper in half and leave slightly folded to give a 3D look.</p>

<div class="cicada"><h2>Flying Cicada</h2>

<p>Step 1) Fold the paper in half.</p>

<p>Step 2) Fold both sides up along the dotted lines.</p>

<p>Step 3) Fold both sides down along the dotted lines.</p>

<p>Step 4) Fold the top layer of paper down along the dotted line.</p>

<p>Step 5) Fold the bottom layer of paper down along the dotted line.</p>

<p>Step 6) Fold the top of the paper down along the dotted line.</p>

<p>Step 7) Fold both sides out along the dotted lines to make the wings.</p>

<p>Step 8) Fold both sides of the paper behind the model.</p>

<p>Step 9) Turn the paper over.</p>

<p>Step 10) Make 3 Pleat Folds on the bottom of the model.</p>
